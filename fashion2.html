Fashion Projects

I'm very excited to share this project I worked on—a really gorgeous purple with a hint of faded blue dress, inspired by a mauve stinger jellyfish.

‍

.Red Beads (Identified as the Stripes of the Jellyfish).

Throughout the dress, I layered and hand sewn these intriguing red beads that resembled the stripes of a jellyfish. They're not just for looks; they add this classy vibe and make the dress stand out.

‍

.Bottom Ruffles Skirt (Identified as the Oral Arms of the Jellyfish).

I created flowing ruffles as the skirt using mesh purple fabric, and I hand stitched every individual piece onto an elastic cord band, making the skirt easier to wear. I thought they'd be perfect as the oral arms of a jellyfish. They move so gracefully, giving the dress this underwater feel.

‍

.Suffolk Puffs (Gives it a More 3D Look and More Under-the-Sea Vibe).

To make the dress stand out, I added this textile technique called suffolk puffs. They're like little 3D details that make it feel more under-the-sea. It's all about texture and making it look alive!

‍

.Cream-Colored Fabric Corset Wrapped with Light Purple Fabric (Texture and Vibrant Look).

The corset part is cream-colored, and I wrapped it up with light purple fabric, which adds texture and makes the top part of the dress look vibrant. I wanted it to be more than just a dress; I wanted it to be an experience.

‍

.Strings of Cut Out Glitter Shower Curtain (Identified as the Tentacles of the Jellyfish).

For the tentacles, I cut out strings from a glitter shower curtain. They dangle down and give off that enchanting underwater vibe. It's like the finishing touch that brings the whole jellyfish look together. The "Purple with a hint of faded blue mauve stinger jellyfish dress" is complete.

‍

However, the black pleated dress with orange 3D printed shapes, which makes the dress stand out, is still a work in progress. Right now, I'm all about trying out different shapes and placements. Some pieces fit perfectly, and others need a bit of tweaking. Feel free to check out my collection of fashion illustrations below!
