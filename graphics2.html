Graphics

The reason behind these dessert posters and the menu is that they're part of my Graphic Art course. For one of my projects, I was tasked with creating visuals for a scenario where I owned my own cafe. creating a cafe experience that focuses on visually striking desserts and a cozy, vintage feel.

Project Overviews

Project Overviews

Graphics Projects

.Promoting the Desserts.

The idea of showcasing these desserts developed as I thought about introducing a new dessert line for my imagined cafe. It is important to attract customers with something fresh and interesting rather than just putting delicious treats on display.

‍

.Fruits and Ice cream to Give the Desserts More Divine Flavours.

Fresh fruits and ice cream became the star ingredients, not just for taste but also to add a touch of wholesomeness to the desserts. The goal was to create treats that were not only visually appealing but also offered a divine flavour experience.

‍

.The Yellow-Colored Font Slogan.

The catchy slogan in vibrant yellow font wasn't just a marketing tactic; it was a way to inject a burst of energy and playfulness into the cafe's brand. It was all about creating a memorable experience, from the visual impact to the "sweet indulgence".

‍

.The Big Bold Yellow Font Title.

The big, bold yellow font title was a deliberate choice to make sure the dessert offerings stood out. It was about making a statement—that this cafe isn't just about desserts; it's about bold flavors and unforgettable moments.

‍

.Cafe Logo - (Brown Theme Gives it Vintage Looks).

The cafe logo, using a brown theme for that vintage aesthetic, became a symbol of the cafe's personality. It was a representation of the blend of tradition and modernity that defined the cafe's identity.
